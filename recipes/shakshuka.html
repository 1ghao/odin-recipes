How to make Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a traditional north african breakfast (although I thought it was turkish). It is very delicious and even though you are going to spend a good amount of time chopping the ingredients if you are as unsharp as me at doing it, you are going be grateful for it.

Ingredients

  • An onion
  • Green, red or both peppers
  • Garlic
  • Coriander
  • Paprika
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Cheese

Steps

  • Chop the garlic, onion and peppers in very small pieces
  • Add olive oil to a pan and wait for it to heat up
  • When the oil is hot, add first the garlic and 5 seconds later the other chopped ingredients
  • Stir fry all the ingredients till golden
  • Add the tomato sauce and stir it
  • Add all the spices to your liking
  • Crack the eggs you want and let it sink with a cover
  • When the eggs are done take the pan out of the heat and serve
  • Eat it accompanied by bread, enjoy!
