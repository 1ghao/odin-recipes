How to make Spaguetti and Meatballs

Description

Spaguetti and meatballs is a simple yet delicious dish that unites families. It is a quick recipe if you just want eat something good and nutricious. Although simple, you will never be dissapointed by its flavour.

Ingredients

  • 100gr of spaguetti per person
  • Meatballs
  • Any cheese that can be gratinated (Ex: parmesan)
  • Half an onion per person
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a pot.
  2. When the water is boiled, put salt in the water till it tastes like seawater.
  3. Put the spaguetti in the water.
  4. Cook the spaguetti till al dente.
  5. In the meantime, put some olive oil in a pan and wait till it's hot.
  6. Chop the onions in small cubes.
  7. Add the onions to the hot olive oil.
  8. Cook the onion till it changes its colour.
  9. Add the tomato sauce to the pan and stir it.
  10. Add salt, black pepper and parsley to your taste.
  11. Lower the heat and leave it there for a while.
  12. Strain the pasta and mix it with the sauce, plate and enjoy your meal!
